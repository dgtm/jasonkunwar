About

Jason Kunwar is a song-writer, lyricist, multi-instrumentalist and music producer and is mostly known as frontman of neo-school folk band Night. He has also composed music for over 10 films and more than 20 short films and documentaries. He is also involved with various theater groups in Nepal where he not only composes music but also performs live.

He is currently working on his solo project Gaau Khaane Kathaa which is slated to be released in 2017.

Upcoming Project

Gau Khane Katha

Padkincha Barood is a song from Jason Kunwar's upcoming album. Gaau Khaane Kathaa is a project where Jason is working with regional artists/folk artists from all around Nepal. He has composed many materials during his fieldwork/music research spanning 6 years and casual tours around Nepal. It features Prakash Gandharva on Sarangi and Vocals. The song was recorded live at Ghyampe Daada, Bhaktapur.

Words and Music : Jason Kunwar
Vocals and Sarangi : Prakash Gandharva
Vocals and Tungna : Jason Kunwar
Field Recordists: Sunit Kansakar and Niraj Shakya
